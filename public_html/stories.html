Stories and Profiles

We are talking to several blind and visually impaired people in math and science across the world and are excited to share their amazing stories with you. We hope that these will inspire you, dispel common myths, take away some of your anxiety, and drive home the fact that blind and visually impaired CAN SUCCESSFULLY PURSUE A CAREER IN THE SCIENCES. You will also be able to reach out to these individuals for guidance and mentorship. All of that, coming soon!

If you are a blind or visually impaired student or professional who has successfully pursued STEM, please get in touch at info@inclusivestem.org. We'd love to speak with you.
